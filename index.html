THE KHUSHII STORY

If there is a vaccine against poverty, it’s education

KHUSHII was set up by cricketing icon, Kapil Dev, in 2003, to help children transform their lives through education. That’s why KHUSHII works across at a national level to ensure that children complete their schooling. Additionally, we help them stay healthy and empower their families economically, so children can stay focused on learning.
